Our Story

From Our Family Farm to Your Table

Founded in 1995, Paradise is Here began as a small family orchard in the heart of Central Russia. What started as a passion project to grow the most flavorful fruits using traditional methods has blossomed into a beloved destination for fruit enthusiasts.

We believe in sustainable farming practices that respect nature's balance. Our fruits are grown without synthetic pesticides or artificial enhancers, allowing their natural flavors to shine through.

Every piece of fruit we offer is hand-picked at peak ripeness, ensuring you experience nature's sweetness exactly as intended.

Our Signature Fruits
